THE XL

THE XL

THE XL

The XL has a large queen size bed, all of the amenities offered in our other rooms and the added benefit of a desk, couch, closet and seating area. The XL suites boast a floor to ceiling window overlooking daily life on Queen West. If you would like to add a few more to the booking, the XL has a Murphy Sofa Bed with a full size Endy mattress.

While you’re figuring out how the CN tower got so tall, order a wake up call or find out about the best restaurants near you through our Volo! tablets, available in every room. The Beverley’s streamlined aesthetic shines through The XL from the material choices right down to the full art wall murals. If you’re looking to see the absolute best of downtown and need a bit more space during your stay, book one of these rooms early—they go fast.

Room Size: 325 Sq ft / 30 Sq m (Comfortable for 2 people, can sleep up to 4)

THE STUDIO

The Studio is the perfect crash pad and an ideal room for the solo traveller. With a comfortable double bed, a view overlooking Queen West, and all the amenities you need; The Studio has everything you could ever want for a stay in the heart of downtown Toronto. The Studio has free and fast WiFi, a flat-screen television, and a mini fridge.

While you are figuring out how to navigate the streetcars, order a wake up call or find out about the best restaurants near you through our Volo! tablet, available in every room. Enjoy all of the minimalistic and sleek design elements exclusive to The Beverley, without sacrificing the essentials.
